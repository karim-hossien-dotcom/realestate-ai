## Sending Strategy

1. **Build list**: Use Apollo.io, LinkedIn Sales Navigator, or Reonomy to find commercial RE brokerages in target markets (NJ, NY, FL, TX, CA).
2. **Personalize**: Replace {FirstName} and add 1 line about their brokerage/recent deal.
3. **Cadence**: Email 1 → wait 3 days → Email 2 → wait 5 days → Email 3.
4. **Volume**: 20-25 per day to avoid spam filters. Use Instantly.ai or Smartlead for warm-up.
5. **Track**: Monitor open rates (target >40%) and reply rates (target >5%).
