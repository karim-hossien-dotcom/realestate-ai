### Compliance Is Not an Afterthought

A common concern with WhatsApp outreach at scale is compliance. Estate AI handles this built-in.

Every outbound WhatsApp message includes a clear opt-out mechanism. Replies containing "STOP" or equivalent language are automatically processed — the lead is added to the DNC (Do Not Contact) list and removed from all future campaigns across every channel. The DNC list is enforced at the API level before any message is sent, not as a post-send filter.

WhatsApp Business API messages require explicit opt-in and template approval from Meta, which means the channel is inherently more compliant than cold SMS or email by design. Estate AI layers on top of this with TCPA-aligned consent tracking and a full audit log of every message sent and received.
