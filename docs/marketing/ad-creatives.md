## Campaign Structure

### Budget Allocation

**Total Monthly Test Budget: $3,000/mo**

| Ad | Type | Budget | Rationale |
|----|------|--------|-----------|
| Ad 1 — Pain Point | Cold traffic | $700/mo | Broad reach, high-intent hook — top of funnel |
| Ad 2 — Social Proof | Cold + warm | $500/mo | Credibility layer for mid-funnel consideration |
| Ad 3 — Demo Preview | Cold + retargeting | $600/mo | Strongest product ad — allocate more |
| Ad 4 — Feature Carousel | Retargeting only | $500/mo | Works on warm audiences who need more detail |
| Ad 5 — Before/After | Retargeting only | $400/mo | Emotional close for fence-sitters |
| Retargeting Pool | All retargeting ads | $300/mo | Separate retargeting campaign budget |

**Budget Scaling Rule:** If any ad achieves CPA below $80 in the first 2 weeks, increase that ad's budget by 50% and reduce the lowest-performing ad by the same amount.

---

### A/B Testing Strategy

**Round 1 — Headline Testing (Week 1–2)**
Run two headline variants per ad. Keep all other variables identical.

| Ad | Variant A (Control) | Variant B (Test) |
|----|---------------------|------------------|
| Ad 1 | "Still texting leads yourself?" | "Your lead just called your competitor." |
| Ad 2 | "40% more conversions. Zero extra work." | "Close more. Work less. Here's the math." |
| Ad 3 | "This is your AI agent working." | "Watch AI close your lead at 2 AM." |

Winner = lower CPC after 500 impressions per variant minimum.

**Round 2 — Visual Testing (Week 3–4)**
Lock winning headlines. Test two visual directions per ad.

| Ad | Variant A | Variant B |
|----|-----------|-----------|
| Ad 1 | Split-screen (described above) | Single hero image: phone showing AI reply with "47 sec" timestamp overlay |
| Ad 3 | Dashboard mockup (MacBook 3/4 angle) | Mobile-only view (iPhone with WhatsApp thread) |

Winner = lower CPA (cost per signup click).

**Round 3 — CTA Button Testing (Week 5–6)**
Test two CTAs on top-performing ad from Rounds 1–2.

| Variant A | Variant B |
|-----------|-----------|
| "Try Free" | "Start Free Trial" |
| "See How It Works" | "Get a Demo" |
| "Watch the Demo" | "See It Live" |

**Ongoing — Audience Testing**
After Round 1, test one new audience segment per month:
- Month 2: Add "eXp Realty" and "SOTHEBYS International Realty" as interest targets
- Month 3: Test 1% Lookalike Audience from email list (requires 100+ subscriber minimum)
- Month 4: Test job title targeting vs. interest-only targeting isolated

---

### KPI Targets

| Metric | Target | Notes |
|--------|--------|-------|
| CTR (Click-Through Rate) | Greater than 1.5% | Industry avg for SaaS B2B is 0.9% — target above |
| CPC (Cost Per Click) | Less than $3.50 | Real estate tools avg $4–6, room to optimize |
| Landing Page CVR | Greater than 8% | % of ad clicks that start signup flow |
| CPA (Cost Per Acquisition) | Less than $90 | Trial signup — target below LTV/CAC ratio |
| ROAS (at 90 days) | Greater than 3x | $1 ad spend should return $3+ in subscription revenue |
| Frequency Cap | 3 impressions/user/week | Avoid ad fatigue, especially for retargeting |
| Trial-to-Paid Rate | Greater than 25% | Benchmark for product stickiness |

**Tracking Requirements:**
- Meta Pixel installed on realestate-ai.app with `Purchase` and `CompleteRegistration` events firing
- UTM parameters on all ad URLs: `utm_source=facebook&utm_medium=paid&utm_campaign=[ad-name]`
- Supabase `daily_reports` entry from marketing agent each Monday with weekly ad performance summary
- Connect ad spend data to `/api/admin/revenue` context so finance can calculate blended CAC

---

### Recommended Launch Sequence

1. Week 1: Launch Ad 1 (Pain Point) + Ad 3 (Demo Preview) — cold traffic only. Build pixel data.
2. Week 2: Launch Ad 2 (Social Proof) in parallel. Begin A/B headline tests.
3. Week 3: Add Ad 4 (Feature Carousel) to retargeting campaign once pixel has 200+ events.
4. Week 4: Add Ad 5 (Before/After) to retargeting. Run Round 2 visual tests.
5. Month 2: Optimize toward winning variants. Begin LAL audience testing if subscriber list is sufficient.

**Note:** All ad copy referencing "40% more conversions" must be reviewed by legal before publishing to confirm it can be substantiated with internal data or amended to "up to 40%." See `docs/legal/` for compliance review process.
